# CrewAI - Competitive Research Agent

## Project Overview

An AI-powered Competitive Research Agent built using CrewAI Studio (No-Code) to automate competitor analysis and generate structured business reports.

The project demonstrates how AI agents can collaborate to perform market research, analyze information, and create professional competitive briefs.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional competitive research requires significant manual effort:
- Collecting competitor information
- Analyzing strengths and weaknesses
- Tracking recent developments
- Preparing business reports

The goal was to automate this process using AI agents.

---

## Solution

Built a multi-agent AI workflow using CrewAI Studio where different AI agents collaborate sequentially:

1. 1. Market Researcher Agent collects competitor information using Web Search.
2. Business Analyst analyzes research findings.
3. Report Writer generates a structured competitive brief.

---

## Agent Architecture

### 1. Market Researcher Agent

**Role:** Competitive Research Specialist

**Goal:**  
Collect latest information about competitors including products, services, market position, and recent updates.

**Tool Used:**
- Web Search

---

### 2. Business Analyst Agent

**Role:** Business Analyst

**Goal:**  
Analyze collected information and identify business insights, strengths, and weaknesses.

**Tool Used:**
- None

---

### 3. Business Report Writer Agent

**Role:** Technical Business Report Writer

**Goal:**  
Generate a professional competitive research report.

**Tool Used:**
- None

---

## Workflow

The AI agents work in a sequential workflow:
Market Researcher
|
↓ 
Business Analyst
|
↓
Business Report Writer

Competitive Brief Report
